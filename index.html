Our Foundation

Welcome to BICTDA - the Borno State Information and Communication Technology Development Agency. Established on March 8, 2024, our mission is to drive digital transformation in Borno State, fostering social and economic growth through enhanced digital access, improved infrastructure, and innovative solutions.
